About H2petrons

H2petrons is a donor-powered engineering lab focused on open-wheel motorsport research. We explore aerodynamics, mechanical systems, and advanced simulation tools to push the limits of performance—while sharing knowledge openly where possible.

Our mission is to democratize access to F1-grade engineering knowledge through open data, tutorials, and community-driven research projects.
